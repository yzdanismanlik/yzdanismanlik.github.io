Work Areas

Our group focuses on a wide range of areas in artificial intelligence, data science, and big data technologies.

Artificial Intelligence

Design of intelligent systems for decision-making, automation, and prediction in real-world domains.

Machine Learning

Development of supervised and unsupervised models for classification, clustering, and regression tasks.

Deep Learning

Neural network architectures for image recognition, speech processing, and natural language understanding.

Computer Vision

Image and video analysis for recognition, tracking, and gesture/lip movement modeling.

Image Processing

Techniques for enhancing, filtering, and segmenting digital images to extract meaningful features for analysis.

Natural Language Processing

Text and speech processing including sentiment analysis, text classification, and translation systems.

Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Generating natural human-like speech from text, enabling applications in virtual assistants and accessibility.

Speech-to-Text (STT)

Automatic transcription of spoken language into written text for dialogue systems and multimodal AI.

Time Series Analysis

Forecasting dynamic data such as finance, healthcare signals, and multimodal human behavior.

Big Data Technologies

Distributed systems and frameworks like Hadoop and Spark for large-scale data processing.
